AirBnB Plan+

AirBnB Plan+ has the user fill out a short questionnaire in order to get a sample itinerary. The target users are those who do not have time to plan or do not like planning. In testing, we found that one the most difficult parts that people found about traveling is the planning. This function strives to make planning a quicker and more enjoyable experience.

AirBnB Traveler Type

AirBnB Traveler Type prompts the user to choose a traveler type in order to generate listings and experiences that cater to that traveler type. This would be helpful for users who may not know which neighborhood to stay in so that their needs are accommodated and they are around like minded people.
